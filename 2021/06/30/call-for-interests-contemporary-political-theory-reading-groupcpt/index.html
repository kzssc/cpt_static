Call for Interests: Contemporary Political Theory Reading Group(CPT)

  • by vpc
  • in Uncategorized
  • on June 30, 2021
0

The CPT is organised by graduates of the University of Warwick. It is a weekly, on-line, pre-read reading group which tackles the most rigorous journal articles (including but not limited to, Ethics1, Philosophy and Public Affairs2, Journal of Political Philosophy3 and Utilitas4) in Political Theory in recent times (10 years).

We encourage like-minded scholars to join us in this hopefully fruitful and enriching experience in understanding, debating and dissecting contemporary debates in political theory.

  1. We foresee equal chances for participants to discuss during CPT, we expect all participants to pre-read the article and come to CPT sessions prepared for a rigorous discussion.
  2. We follow a voluntary sign-up of chair5, this entails duties and obligations as follows:
    1. Proposal of a suitable paper in above journals for reading and discussion, 7 days before the session takes place;
    2. To give a 15 min presentation at the beginning of CPT meetings on the main argument of the reading, as well as posing leading questions for discussions;
    3. Chair the session with a hand+finger system – while participating in the discussion.
  3. Observers are allowed. An observer is a participant of CPT who opted to not undertake only the chair’s duties and obligations (clause 2): observers are still fully expected to participate into CPT discussions (non-clause 2);
  4. CPT meets on Microsoft Teams weekly;
  5. CPT’s working language is English. 6

To join, please send an email7 to contemporarypoliticaltheory+subscribe(at)googlegroups.com, a subject or body is not required.

Do not forget to whitelist the email address “contemporarypoliticaltheory(at)googlegroups.com” to avoid disappointment.

CPT’s session is on every Wednesday 10-12pm (GMT+8)

All queries go to chris.zhang(at)upf.edu.
