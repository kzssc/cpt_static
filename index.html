What is CPT?

CPT is a weekly, on-line, pre-read reading group. We tackle central philosophical texts and engage in deep discussions. Participants are expected to read the texts in advance to ensure a fruitful dialogue.
CPT是一个以当代政治理论研究为兴趣而聚集起来的读书小组，以阅读英文文献为主，参与者是相关领域的高校老师和学生。

How it Works

Discussions are conducted in Mandarin Chinese and English. Presenters lead with a 30-minute introduction followed by questions. Meetings are held weekly, at 8-10 pm GMT+8. See what we have read here.

讨论以普通话和英语进行。主讲人先进行30分钟的介绍，然后是问答环节。会议每周举行，时间是格林尼治标准时间+8区的晚上8点至10点。请参阅我们阅读过的内容。
